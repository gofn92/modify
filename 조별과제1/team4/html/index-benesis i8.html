Electric power alone can travel up to 24 km and enjoy the thrill of sports driving on the outskirts. Combining the advantages of electricity and internal combustion into one: Gasoline engines and electric motors

that these two technical combinations are never possible. When the eDrive button is pressed, the BMW i8 is driven purely by electricity.

In Comfort, ECO PRO mode, you can drive only by electricity. If you select Comfort mode on eDrive, you will only drive on pure electricity without using an internal combustion engine until the battery is discharged. The electric motor alone can drive at speeds up to 120 km/h. When Comfort or ECO PRO mode is not selected, the internal combustion engine is automatically enabled at 60 km/h. *These figures are the results of Benesis's own fuel economy research.
